  Suggested strategy: 

Read 3.1, 3.2, and 3.3  before working on the Conjugation exercise and the Drill on present and future. 

Before reading 3.4, review, if necessary, 2.4.  Print the two charts  in Rev 1.3 to use as models while you practice with the Drill on the 2nd declension.

Read the Translation while listening to the soundfile, then read it aloud on your own.  Analyze the syntax before translating, and later check the Key.  It is useful to read the Greek text of the translation again after modifying any errors in your first interpretation.  The composition will serve to consolidate what you have learned in this lesson.  Feel free to look up the Vocabularies as you do both Translation and Composition.  You will remember the underscored words easily if you review them in their context, rather than trying to memorize their citations mechanically.

The quiz should be fun now.  Submit it, and when you receive the key, perhaps with your instructor's comments, print out the quiz again and do it once more!
