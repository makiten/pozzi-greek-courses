      Quiz for Lesson 6                         

 Suggested strategy: 

a) 6.1 should be followed by the drill.

b) In 6.2a and 6.2b it is suggested that you learn the primary and secondary endings as best you can.  This will avoid lots of pure memorization later, as you will be able to construct new past tenses as you learn them.  The practice for these two pages is essential.

c) 6.3 brings together the few invariable forms introduced so far.  Use translation and composition exercises as thoughtfully as possible.
