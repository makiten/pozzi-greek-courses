NOUNS, PRONOUNS & ADJECTIVES HAVE GENDER, NUMBER & CASE

YOU HAVE STUDIED 

THREE GENDERS:     MASCULINE      FEMININE       NEUTER  

TWO NUMBERS:        SINGULAR        PLURAL        

FIVE CASES:        NOMINATIVE        GENITIVE        DATIVE        ACCUSATIVE        VOCATIVE

VERBS HAVE:  PERSON, NUMBER, TENSE, MOOD, VOICE

YOU HAVE STUDIED 

THREE PERSONS IN THE SINGULAR:     1) = I      2) = YOU       3) = HE, SHE, IT  

THREE PERSONS IN THE PLURAL 

FOUR TENSES:        PRESENT         FUTURE        IMPERFECT        AORIST        

TWO MOODS:         INDICATIVE      IMPERATIVE

THREE VOICES:       ACTIVE            MIDDLE          PASSIVE
