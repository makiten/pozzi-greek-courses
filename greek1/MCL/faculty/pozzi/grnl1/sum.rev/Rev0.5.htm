REVIEW 0.5: Parts of Speech

 

NOUNS name

PRONOUNS replace nouns

ADJECTIVES modify nouns or pronouns

VERBS:    

LINKING VERBS connect subject and a noun, adjective, pronoun, etc. in the predicate 

PREDICATIVE VERBS express an action or a condition.  They may be transitive (able to take a direct object) or intransitive (normally no D O)

ADVERBS modify verbs, adjectives, other adverbs

PREPOSITIONS join nouns to other parts of speech, forming "prepositional phrases"

CONJUNCTIONS connect words or groups of words.  They may join clauses otherwise independent from one another (coordinating conjunctions) or they may make one clause dependent upon another (subordinating conjunctions).
