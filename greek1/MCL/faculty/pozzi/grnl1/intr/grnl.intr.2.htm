Suggested strategy: 

Read from the screen or print out 0.2.1, then click on the links to vowels and to the vowels and syllables and listen to the soundfiles.

Read or print out the three "Read Greek" pages and practice pronunciation listening to the soundfiles.

Read 0.2.2  Pronounce each letter to make sure that you understand the classification of sounds.  Remembering the classification of "stops" into labial, dental, and palatal, will become useful in Lesson 3.  You will remember more of this classification as you return to the page when we refer to it in studying the grammar.

Read the English columns in 0.2.3.  As you learn new parts of speech in Greek, you may return to this chart for reference.
