12.2b = 13.3  Overview of dependent clauses

 

The functions of dependent clauses with respect to a main clause upon which they depend may be paralleled to the functions of three parts of speech in a simple clause:  nouns, adjectives, and adverbs.  Classifying dependent clauses accordingly allows us to perceive more easily the structure of complex periods.  The examples on this page are in English.  In this lesson I present one of these types: noun clauses, in 12.3, 12.4a and Rev2.11.
