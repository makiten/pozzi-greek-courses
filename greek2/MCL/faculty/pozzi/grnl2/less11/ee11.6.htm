PRONOUNS OR ADJECTIVES

a) The numeral  εἷς, μία, ἕν (= one)  may be used as a pronoun or an adjective. Its compound  οὐδείς, οὐδεμία, οὐδέν ( =  no one) has a similar declension.  So does μηδείς, μηδεμία, μηδέν. The general stem in the masculine and neuter, which belong to the 3rd declension, is  ἑν-  /  οὐδεν-, and the feminine belongs to the 1st declension.  Neither "one" nor "no one," of course, has a plural.
