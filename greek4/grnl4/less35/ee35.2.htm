35.2: Resources to study Biblical Greek

 

TEXTS AND SEARCH ENGINES

 

·           Perseus, a magnificent multimedia site with abundant materials for the study of the ancient Greek language and culture and much more. It offers online a monumental array of searchable texts, translations, search engines, links to scholarly textbooks, plus a world of information on history, geography, culture, and a marvelous art and architecture database.  Students of Biblical Greek will only find the text of the Old Testament in English and of the New Testament in Greek and English.

 

·           For the Septuagint in Greek as well as for the New Testament, Bible.Gateway.com is useful.  It facilitates access to texts with a complete table with separate links to books and chapters of the Bible.

 

·           A reliable website for the New Testament: the New Testament Gateway (University of Birmingham), with numerous resources.

 

·           Crosswalk had Bible Study tools, with searchable OT and NT texts, and with many translations that can be compared side by side.  It addresses ministers and students.  Used selectively (beware of the interlinear translations!) it helps.
